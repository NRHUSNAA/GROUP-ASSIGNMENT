Leeyanarahman

LEEYANARAHMAN is an award-winning Muslim modest wear designer and producer based in Bandar Baru Bangi, Selangor, Malaysia. With customer in several neighbouring Nusantara countries, LEEYANARAHMAN has sparked the trend within the fashion industry, to make modern fashion accessible to Muslims and fans of modest wear around the world.

Elrah Exclusive

The Elrah Exclusive clothing line is a well known local brand that offers stylish Baju Melayu and Kurta for men. The company was founded by the entrepreneur couple, El Azman and his wife, Nur Amirah.

Amalia Exclusive

Amalia Exclusive is fully designed by Amalia Tailoring House. It has been managed by Amalia Resources with the extra quality of material and measurement. They do emphasize for the services of cutting and sewn as per the fitting and body measurements. A little tailoring can go a long way in the world of affordable off-the-rack clothing
